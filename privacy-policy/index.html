Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2024

At The Batraa Numerology, protecting your personal information is our top priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard the information you share with us when you interact with our services.

1. Information We Collect

We gather information necessary for providing our services, including:

  • Full name and contact details (email address, phone number)
  • Date of birth and other numerology-relevant inputs
  • Payment information (processed securely via Razorpay — we do not store card details)
  • Usage preferences when you sign up for consultations or workshops

2. How We Use Your Information

Information collected is used solely for delivering personalised numerology services and enhancing your experience. Specifically, we use your data to:

  • Prepare and deliver your numerology report or workshop access
  • Communicate service updates and respond to your queries
  • Improve the quality of our offerings over time

3. Data Protection

We employ robust security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted during transmission and storage
  • Access Control: Only authorised personnel can access sensitive data
  • Regular Audits: We periodically review our practices to ensure compliance with security standards

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties, except when legally required or strictly necessary to fulfil a service (for example, payment processing via Razorpay).

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or request deletion of your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights, please contact us at thebatraanumerology@gmail.com.

6. Updates to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this policy as needed. Users will be notified of any significant changes via email or a notice on our website.
